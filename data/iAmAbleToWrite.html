Spring season, Good internet connection and Freedom

One of the reason that I am able to write, right now, it could be spring
season. Spring season is next to the winter season (abviosly) and it seems
by changing the environment, people change too (abviosly).
I don't know how internet connection is connected to my mode of writing.
What I know is if I am not in a good mode of writing then there is high
chance of not having a good internet connection too.
Freedom have levels (I think) and by writing something you could change
that level of freedom for yourself and/or for others.
Well you may know why you (talking to myself) are able to write, right now.

Writing for myself

I am writing things down for myself. I founded it useful when I have a
repeated task. For example once I wrote a post in my blog about using some
software program in my current project. Later on I back to that post to use
it for another project. Simply I was not able to remmeber required steps.
Another advantage for me is, it shows some of my thinking about particualr
subject. And when I am backing to that subject in my future way then I
could use those forgotton points.

Is our mind a safe place for our thinking?

We don't know about full potential of the brain. Some day maybe not far
we may find a way to use more of this potentail. Until that day I will use
computer hard drive as an safe place to leave my thinking for my future
access.

advise (talking to myself) since you are thinking about making a good
